Get to Know Me

Who is Jeanine Meli?

I am a third year student at the University of Malta, studying for my BFA in Digital Arts. I mainly enjoy working in graphic design and illustration. I love experimenting and discovering new methods to work in. My illustration style varies from detailed works, portraits, homours works, dot and line work to also some fashion and streetware illustrations.

I have experience in graphic design and have had some commissions for things such as: logos, stickers, tags, brochures, menus, packaging and illustrations for branding.

In the upcoming future I wish to pursue my love for illustraion through book design and well as continue working in graphic design.
